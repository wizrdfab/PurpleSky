Endpoint: GET /v5/market/kline
Description: Historical klines (candlesticks).
Params:
- category: linear (default), spot, inverse
- symbol: e.g. BTCUSDT
- interval: 1, 3, 5, 15, 30, 60, 120, 240, 360, 720, D, M, W
- limit: [1, 1000]
Response:
- list: [[startTime, open, high, low, close, volume, turnover], ...]
